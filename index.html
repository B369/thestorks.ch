Welcome to our vibrant world, your The Strokes tribute band! Hailing from Switzerland, we bring the iconic sound and electrifying energy of The Strokes to life on stage. Our band is dedicated to capturing the raw, edgy spirit and infectious melodies that have made The Strokes a defining force in modern rock.

From the gritty, garage-rock vibes of "Is This It" to the polished yet punchy sounds of "Angles" and beyond, we cover the full spectrum of The Strokes’ discography. Our setlists are carefully crafted to include all the fan favorites, from the anthemic "Last Nite" and "Someday" to the exhilarating "Reptilia" and "Hard to Explain." We ensure every note, riff, and lyric resonates with the same intensity and emotion that The Strokes are known for.

The Strokes are an American rock band, formed in New York City in 1999. The Strokes discography consists of six studio albums, seventeen singles and twenty music videos.

Is This It, their the debut album, is the most influential, most important, and perhaps even the greatest rock album of the past 20 years. The bands and musicians that it inspired, the effect the members themselves had on fashion and culture, the cross-continental acclaim, the endless critical praise, and now the numerous retrospectives solidifying its divinity can all be cited as evidence.
